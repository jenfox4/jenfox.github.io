About Me

I am a full stack developer with a background in STEM education and a deep curiosity for all things math and science. As a Computer Science teacher with a love for learning, I taught myself how to code through online resources. While teaching myself, I quickly became passionate about the logic and problem-solving of coding and fascinated with the incredible possibilities of tech. I soon co-led the Robotics team and founded and led Girls Who Code. I worked on developing a comprehensive computer science curriculum at my school. Although I love developing curiosity in others, I quickly realized that my true passion was in coding and creating through the media of code. I decided to do the General Assembly full immersion career accelerator to learn the latest front-end and back-end tools and technologies and learn more about the computer programming industry. I can happily say taking this leap was one of the best decisions I could have made. I feel as though I have truly found my passion.

I am currently applying to full stack developer positions. As a previous educator, I have multiple skills that transfer to the tech industry including leadership, organization, attention to detail, and perseverance through problem solving. I consider myself a go-getter with a lot of grit and I accept challenges with optimism. I am seeking to work on a collobrative and growth mindset team at an innovative company whose mission seeks to better our world.
