About Me

I am a full stack developer with a background in STEM education and a deep curiosity for all things math and science. Graduating from the Penn State honors college in 2014, I decided to join Teach for America in Chicago as I deeply believe in their mission to create equitable educational opportunity for all students across America. For two years I taught math and science in West Chicago and earned a Master degree in education from Dominican University.

After Teach For America, I decided to pursue one of my life long dreams of venturing the world. In 8 months I went to over 4 continents and 20 countries. I had the opportunity to teach in Australia and in Spain. I'm a huge outdoors enthusiast and fulfilled my dream of trekking to Mount Everest and scuba diving the Great Barrier Reef.

Upon my return to the U.S. I decided to move to Boston because of their innovation and general sense of collaboration. I took a position as a Computer Science, Math, and Science teacher at a innovative school in Boston. As a Computer Science teacher with a love for learning, I taught myself how to code through online resources. While teaching myself, I quickly became passionate about the logic and problem-solving of coding and fascinated with the incredible possibilities of tech. I soon co-led the Robotics team and founded and led Girls Who Code. I worked on developing a comprehensive computer science curriculum at my school.

Although I love developing curiosity in others, I quickly realized that my true passion was in coding and creating through the medium of code. I decided to do the General Assembly full immersion career accelerator to learn the latest front-end and back-end tools and technologies and learn more about the computer programming industry. I can happily say taking this leap was one of the best decisions I could have made. I feel as though I have truly found my passion.

I am currently applying to full stack developer positions. As a previous educator, I have multiple skills that transfer to the tech industry including leadership, organization, attention to detail, and perseverance through problem solving. I consider myself a go-getter with a lot of grit and I accept challenges with optimism. I am seeking to work on a collaborative and growth mindset team at an innovative company whose mission seeks to better our world.

Flash Vocab: A GRE Vocab Flashcard App

Flash vocab is an app where students on the go can study GRE flashcards. The app starts with over 1,000 GRE words to study from. Add words to your own personal deck that you need to study or practice new words at random. This is a full-stack single page web application built with JavaScript, HTML, CSS/Sass, Handlebars, and AJAX on the client side. It supports all user authentication and all CRUD actions. The back-end was built utilizing test-driven development with Ruby/ Ruby on rails, PostgreSQL, and Heroku. User stories, wireframes, and entity relationship diagrams are available on github.
