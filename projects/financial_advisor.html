Financial Advisor

Albeit basic, this task shows my self-learning attitude and ability to figure out task on my own. Having only a few weeks of a python course through Edx, this project was in conjunction with the MIT online course Introduction to Computer Programming. I was tasked with three different problems to calculate debt payments. The reason I am showcasing this particular problem solving tasks is due to it's progression of challenge, which demonstrate my ability to evolve with the task at hand, but also because I found this task particularly interesting and relevant. Although I have no formal education in finance, I love learning about economics and finance and find scripts that coincide with finance problems to be fascinating.
